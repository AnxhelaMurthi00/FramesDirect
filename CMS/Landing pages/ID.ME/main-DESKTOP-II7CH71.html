Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an ID.me account?

Simply visit ID.me and follow the instructions to create your free account. You’ll just need some basic documentation to verify your status.

How do I get the FramesDirect discount through ID.me?
  1. Add at least $75 in frames to your cart.
  2. At checkout, choose to verify with ID.me.
  3. Once verified, your 10% discount will be automatically applied.

Note: Discount is combinable with vision insurance.

What is ID.me?

ID.me is a secure and trusted platform that helps verify your eligibility for exclusive discounts. At FramesDirect, we proudly offer a 10% discount to select groups as a thank-you for their service..

Who is eligible for the ID.me discount?

The discount is available to:

  • Military (active duty, veterans, spouses, and dependents)
  • First responders (police, firefighters, EMTs, etc.)
  • Teachers and school staff
  • Medical professionals
  • Government employees
  • Students (enrolled in a US-accredited college or university)
  • Seniors (age 65 and over)

For a full list, visit ID.me’s eligibility page.

I’m having trouble with ID.me—how can I get help?

You can visit the ID.me Help Center or chat directly with a support agent for quick assistance.

Is the ID.me discount available outside the US?

Currently, ID.me verification is only available to eligible US-based customers.
