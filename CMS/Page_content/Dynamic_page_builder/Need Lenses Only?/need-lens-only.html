Need Lenses Only? How to Order Prescription Lenses for Glasses You Already Own

If you already have a pair of glasses you love but need new prescription lenses, you don’t have to buy new frames. Ordering lenses only is a practical option that lets you keep your existing frames while upgrading your vision.

Can You Buy Prescription Lenses Only for Glasses?

Yes. You can order prescription lenses only for glasses you already own, as long as the frames are eligible. This option is ideal if you have frames but need new lenses due to an updated prescription, scratched lenses, or normal wear—without replacing the entire pair of glasses.

Who Should Order Lenses Only?

Ordering lenses for frames you already have is a good solution if any of the following apply:

You Have Frames but Need New Prescription Lenses:

If your vision has changed and you need a new prescription, you can keep your current frames and replace only the lenses.

Your Lenses Are Scratched or Damaged:

Scratches, coating wear, or cracks can affect vision quality. Replacing the lenses restores clarity without buying new glasses.

You Want to Keep Frames You Love:

If your frames are discontinued, customized, or simply your favorite, ordering lenses only for glasses helps you keep the exact style and fit.

What Types of Prescription Lenses Can You Order?

You can order custom prescription lenses for most everyday vision needs, including:

Single Vision, Bifocal, and Progressive Lenses

Replacement are available for distance, reading, or multifocal correction, including progressive lenses.

Lens Materials and Coatings

Options may include lightweight materials, anti-reflective coatings, blue light filtering, and UV protection.

Prism Lenses

If your prescription includes prism correction, prism lenses can be added when ordering lenses only, subject to frame compatibility.

How to Order Lenses for Glasses You Already Own

Follow these steps to order prescription lenses only online for your existing frames.

Step 1: Confirm Your Frames Are Eligible

Contact an optician to verify that your exact frame model, color, and size are currently supported.

Step 2: Choose Your Prescription Lenses

An optician can help you select the correct lenses based on your prescription, lifestyle, and visual needs.

Step 3: Send Your Frames Securely

Mail your frames along with:

  • Your valid prescription
  • A signed frame waiver

Frames must be shipped via FedEx or UPS with signature required and insurance. USPS is not recommended.

Step 4: Lens Installation and Return Shipping

Once your frames and prescription are received, the order is confirmed and lenses are installed before shipping your glasses back to you.

Frequently Asked Questions About Ordering Lenses Only

How do I know which lenses to choose?
An optician can review your prescription and recommend lens types and coatings based on your vision needs and daily activities.
Will insurance cover replacement lenses?
Some vision insurance plans may cover part of the cost of replacement lenses. Coverage depends on your provider and plan details.
How long does it take to complete a lenses-only order?
Turnaround time varies depending on prescription complexity and shipping, but orders typically take several business days after frames are received.
What if my frames are no longer under warranty?
Sending frames always carries some risk, especially with older or fragile frames. A signed waiver is required before processing.
Can I add prism to my lenses?
Yes, prism lenses can be added when ordering lenses only, provided your frames are compatible with the prescription requirements.
