Eyewear Protection Plan FAQs

How long is the term of the Eyewear Protection Plan?

Our Eyewear Protection Plan is a 12-month program.

Will the Eyewear Protection Plan provide a repair for my eyewear?

No. The Eyewear Protection Plan will replace your eyewear for covered breakdowns.

How often can I have my eyewear replaced under the Eyewear Protection Plan?

Our Eyewear Protection Plan covers a one-time replacement of your eyewear for a covered breakdown during the 12-month term.

Are there any fees when filing a claim?

Yes, when filing a claim, there is a flat replacement fee of $50 for most eyewear. For Ray-Ban Meta, the one-time replacement fee is $100.

Does the Eyewear Protection Plan cover loss or theft?

No. The Eyewear Protection Plan is not insurance and does not cover loss or theft.

What kind of things does the Eyewear Protection Plan cover?

The Eyewear Protection Plan covers cracked and scratched lenses; cracked frames; broken hinges; normal wear and tear. (Limitations and exclusions apply, please refer to the Terms and Conditions for plan details. For customers in Puerto Rico, please see the English and Spanish versions.)

When can I buy the Eyewear Protection Plan?

The Eyewear Protection Plan can only be purchased at the same time you purchase your eyewear from Frames Direct. The Eyewear Protection Plan cannot be added to your eyewear at a later date.

Is the Eyewear Protection Plan renewable?

Not at this time.

How do I file a claim?

It's easy! Just contact Frames Direct's customer service at 800-248-9427 and let the agent know you've purchased an Eyewear Protection Plan. They'll assist you in getting your claim filed as quickly as possible.

If I purchase multiple pairs of eyewear, will I need to buy an Eyewear Protection Plan for each pair?

Yes, each eyewear must have its own Eyewear Protection Plan.

If I file a claim, can I keep my original broken eyewear?

No, the eyewear must be returned to Frames Direct when submitting a claim.

Will the Eyewear Protection Plan replace my eyewear with the exact same style?

Yes, if the style is available. If not, you'll have the option to choose an alternate product.
