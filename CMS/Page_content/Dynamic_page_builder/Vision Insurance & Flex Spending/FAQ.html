Transitions® Light Intelligent Lenses™ FAQs

What are the practical benefits of Transitions® lenses?

Transitions® lenses automatically adapt to changing light conditions by darkening outdoors and returning to clear indoors. This helps provide comfortable vision throughout the day without needing to switch between glasses and sunglasses. They also offer UV protection and are available in a range of colors to suit your personal style.

How do Transitions® lenses work?

Transitions® lenses are light-adaptive lenses that automatically adjust to changing light conditions. They darken when exposed to sunlight and return to clear indoors. This happens thanks to photochromic technology embedded in the lens material. Transitions® lenses also provide 100% UV protection and help filter blue-violet light.*

Are Transitions® lenses available in different styles and colors?

Yes. Transitions® lenses come in multiple color options to match your style and frame choice. Available colors and lens types may vary based on your prescription and selected product.

Do you need a prescription for Transitions® lenses?

No, a prescription is not required for Transitions® lenses. They are available in both prescription and non-prescription options, allowing individuals with or without vision correction needs to enjoy their benefits.

Who can benefit from wearing Transitions® lenses?

Transitions® lenses are a smart choice for those who want one pair that adapts to their day. They seamlessly adjust from indoor to outdoor light, offering added comfort and versatility for people with on-the-go lifestyles.
