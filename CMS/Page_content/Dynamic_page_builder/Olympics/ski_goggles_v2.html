Why Quality Ski Eyewear Matters

Ski eyewear shields against UV snow blindness (up to 80% reflection), wind chill, and debris—critical for athletes in downhill (speeds 80+ mph) or halfpipe jumps. Oakley's Prizm lenses boost contrast by 20% on ice; Von Zipper spherical designs reduce distortion for Olympic-grade vision. Poor eyewear risks crashes; pros choose ANSI Z87.1-rated options.
