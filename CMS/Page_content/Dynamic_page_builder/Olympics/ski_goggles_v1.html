Best Polarized Sunglasses for Skiing: Smith Wildcat

Smith Optics has made a name for themselves for their durable, reliable eyewear. The Smith Wildcat polarized lenses earned them the title of best polarized sunglasses for skiing.

The wraparound frame design is made of TR-90. TR-90 is a durable, lightweight material that keeps its shape in extreme heat or hold. The polarized lenses have mirror coating to help reflect incoming light from the lens.

The purpose of polarization is to reduce glare in ultra-bright conditions, like on a snow-covered ski slope. Certain lens colors are equipped with light-adjusting photochromic lens technology. This technology causes lenses to get darker in bright conditions and lighter in dim conditions. With photochromic lenses, your sunglasses adjust wherever you go.
