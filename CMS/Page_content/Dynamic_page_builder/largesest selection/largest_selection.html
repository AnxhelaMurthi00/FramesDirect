Top Selling Eyewear Brands

Burberry

British maker Burberry pairs precision with luxury: From its trademark trench coat to its iconic check pattern, the Burberry brand epitomizes refinement. Burberry sunglasses and eyeglasses frames showcase the style and quality you expect of this storied designer label.

Shop Burberry
Costa

Costa eyewear is made for water worshipers, by water worshipers. Patented lens technology provides absolute clarity and protection on the water, and the performance frames refuse to slip, rust, or weigh you down. The Costa legacy of innovative design continues in the Untangled line of recycled eyewear made from discarded commercial fishing nets.

Shop Costa
Dolce & Gabbana

Italian designers Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana first carried their cosmopolitan vision to the world in high-end clothing, and later accessories and eyewear. Dolce & Gabbana sunglasses and eyeglasses are unconventional and flavorful, shrugging off classic form in favor of daring design.

Shop Dolce & Gabbana
Giorgio Armani

Italian designer Giorgio Armani, celebrated for his red-carpet couture, offers exquisite options in eyewear. Capturing the refined glamour exclusive to this fashion house, Giorgio Armani eyewear defines A-list style with its clean lines and inspired designs.

Shop Giorgio Armani
Gucci

Gucci eyewear is a beautiful extension to its existing lines of luxury apparel, handbags, and fragrances. The Italian couture design house creates glasses with quality and exquisite style in mind, for the discerning buyer.

Shop Gucci
Kate Spade

The colorful world of Kate Spade began at a boutique in New York City, with a line of handbags. Kate Spade New York became a brand synonymous with trendy, feminine fashion and designer quality. Kate Spade glasses and sunglasses offer the same vibrant style the label has embraced from the beginning.

Shop Kate Spade
Maui Jim

Developed on the Hawaiian island of Maui, Maui Jim sunglasses are designed to eliminate glare and combat UV rays while enhancing colors. The American Skin Cancer Foundation has endorsed Maui Jim sunglasses for offering the highest possible amount of UV protection to the eyes and surrounding skin. Maui Jim eyewear is popular at the beach and on the water for its superior glare-cutting lens features and durable frames.

Shop Maui Jim
Oakley

Oakley eyewear represents the culmination of performance innovation and modern design. The brand continues to pave the way in sports-specific lens technology for both regular and prescription sunglass lenses, and also offers athletic eyeglasses frames for the sports-minded glasses wearer.

Shop Oakley
Persol

A Supreme Arrow symbol at the temple hinge betrays this elegant, artful eyewear as Persol, but it's only the beginning. Behind the timeless beauty of the frames is the brand’s original and innovative design that makes these glasses and sunglasses as comfortable as they are famous: The patented Persol Meflecto flexible stem system allows the temples to adapt to any face shape, reducing pressure at the sides of the head for unsurpassed comfort.

Shop Persol
Prada

A distinctly original Italian designer label, Prada opens the door into a world of cutting-edge fashion. Prada began as an exclusive store specializing in luggage, accessories, and luxury goods, but now the brand offerings include eyewear that echoes its avant-garde approach to style.

Shop Prada
POLO by Ralph Lauren

Ralph Lauren's flagship POLO brand emerged in 1967 as a premium lifestyle label for the clean-cut and well-tailored man. Now, POLO eyeglasses and sunglasses offer tasteful style and superior quality for men and women who prefer American prep-inspired, modern-meets-vintage style.

Shop POLO by Ralph Lauren
Ralph Lauren

Since the 1960s, this leading luxury lifestyle brand has offered sophisticated, classic products. The Ralph Lauren eyewear collection continues the tradition with tailored and timeless eyewear styles, and dependable quality for men and women.

Shop Ralph Lauren
Ray-Ban

Founded in the USA, Ray-Ban originally produced sunglasses for U.S. military pilots. Today, it's one of the world's most celebrated eyewear brands, creating glasses and sunglasses in a variety of frame and lens styles, including the iconic Wayfarer and Aviator silhouettes. Then and now, Ray-Ban glasses nod to impeccable classic style.

Shop Ray-Ban
WileyX

Born out of the U.S. military's need for durable eyewear, Wiley X was founded by a veteran whose goal was creating shatterproof, ballistics-rated glasses for the U.S. Armed Forces. Today, the brand’s durable frames and authentic WX Lab lenses are available for civilians who live life on the edge, hunting, fishing, and riding the open road.

Shop Wiley X
