Insurance Frequently Asked Questions

Frames Direct is an in-network provider for most vision insurance plans. You can submit your prescription eyewear or contact lens purchase for reimbursement from your insurance provider if in-network benefits are included in your vision plan.

Can I use insurance to pay for glasses?
Yes, in most cases, you can use your insurance, including FSA or HSA dollars, to purchase prescription glasses, sunglasses, and contact lenses. Non-prescription contacts and sunglasses may be covered if theyâ€™re prescribed by your doctor.
Can I buy prescription glasses online with insurance on FramesDirect?
Yes, you can use your insurance, including FSA or HSA dollars, to purchase prescription eyewear easily online here at FramesDirect.com. Just add your insurance information at checkout or use your FSA/HSA debit card.
Does FramesDirect.com accept my insurance plan?
We accept many vision insurance plans including EyeMed, Anthem, Aetna Vision Preferred, Humana Vision, and more.
How do I use my insurance benefits to pay for glasses?
It depends on your insurance. If we accept your plan, just add your information at checkout. You can use your FSA or HSA card just like a normal debit card. In other cases, reimbursement forms are available on our website.
