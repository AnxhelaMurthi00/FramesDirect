Where Are Versace Eyeglasses Made?

Versace eyeglasses are made exclusively in Italy. They are designed, manufactured, and distributed by Luxottica. If your Versace eyeglasses are stamped with the name of any other country, they are not authentic.

How Can You Tell Whether Versace Glasses Are Authentic?

If you currently own a pair of Versace eyeglasses, look inside the temples. Authentic Versace eyeglasses are imprinted with the brand name followed by the phrase ‘Made in Italy’ inside the right temple; the model number appears on the inside of the left temple. New eyeglasses also come with a booklet and a hardshell case displaying the Versace name on the front. In addition, Versace provides an online verification system for identifying the authenticity of products sold in their boutiques and at authorized retailers; simply tap the NFC tag on the product label using the app on your smartphone.
