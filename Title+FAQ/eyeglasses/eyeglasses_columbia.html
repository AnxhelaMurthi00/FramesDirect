Columbia Prescription Eyeglasses

In 1938, two German nationals fled the Nazi’s and settled in the Portland area. They bought a small hat company and named it Columbia, after the largest river in the Pacific Northwest. These were the humble beginnings of Columbia Sportswear, now a leading manufacturer of outdoor clothing and accessories.

Columbia has been in the outdoor gear business for over 70 years, and is passionate about providing quality and value to their adventure-loving customers. Now Columbia has created a line of eyeglasses perfect for enjoying the outdoors – whether it’s a neighborhood park or a mountain top.

Columbia glasses are designed for both performance and style and made from durable materials like Acetate and Monel, a metal alloy.
