How Can You Tell if Marc Jacobs Glasses Are Real?

Purchase real Marc Jacobs frames from an authorized retailer like FramesDirect.com to ensure that they are 100% authentic. If you already own a pair of Marc Jacobs glasses and want to know whether they’re the genuine article, start by surveying the frames from the top of the bridge downwards. The glasses should be completely symmetrical, and without irregularities or blemishes. Next, check the interior of the temples. One temple should have the brand name and CE symbol stamped on its interior, and the other should have the sizing and style number. Next, check the logos and labels on the temples and lenses. If you cannot easily remove them with a fingernail, your glasses are most likely real.
