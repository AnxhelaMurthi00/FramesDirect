How Do I Tell if My Vera Bradley Glasses Are Real?

To ensure your Vera Bradley glasses are real, always purchase them from an authorized retailer like FramesDirect.com. If you own or recently purchased Vera Bradley glasses and want to verify their authenticity, we recommend that you first survey them from the top of the bridge downwards. If you can see any sizing irregularities or defects, then the frames are most likely fakes. Next, check the interior of the temples for sizing and other quality assurance information relevant to the glasses. If none of the labels can be gently removed with a fingernail, your Vera Bradley glasses are most likely authentic.
