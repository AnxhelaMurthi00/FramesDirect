What Glasses Are in Style for Women?

The current trends in women’s eyeglasses skew towards bold, classic styles like browline and wayfarer frames, and subtle, minimalist styles like rimless and semi-rimless frames. In fact, the two most popular styles of the year are a pair of rimless glasses from Silhouette and the timeless, browline-style Clubmaster from Ray-Ban. Not only are rimless and browline glasses currently in style, but even when they’re not at the top of the popularity podium, they’re usually in second or third place, which makes them a solid, long-term fashion investment.

Can a Woman Wear Men’s Eyeglasses?

Not only can a woman wear any pair of men’s eyeglasses that fit, women often choose to wear traditional men’s frames. The only rule in eyewear is that your glasses should fit, and the only key difference between women’s and men’s glasses (besides colors and some shapes) is the frame size. So as long as the men’s frames fit comfortably, a woman can wear them with her own prescription.
