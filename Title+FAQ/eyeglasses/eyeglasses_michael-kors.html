How Do You Tell If Michael Kors Glasses Are Real?

The best way to make sure that your Michael Kors Glasses are 100% authentic is to purchase them from an authorized retailer like FramesDirect.com. If you already own a pair of Michael Kors eyeglasses but have questions about their authenticity, first survey your new glasses from the top of the bridge down. If any of the components looks warped or is sized incorrectly, there is a good chance the frames are fake. If they look perfectly symmetrical, and none of the labels or logos can be easily removed with a fingernail, then check the interior of the temples. Authentic Michael Kors glasses will have the style number preceded by a clearly printed MK on the inside arm of the glasses.
