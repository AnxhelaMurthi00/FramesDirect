XXL: Glasses That Fit

The frames in the XXL glasses collection come in men's sizes between 55 and 63mm, with a wider bridge—the distance between the lenses—to prevent slipping and pinching. The wider rims allow your eyes to be positioned correctly in the lenses, for improved optical clarity. The longer temple arms provide a better fit on a larger head and prevent discomfort behind the ears. And because XXL Eyewear approaches design with a focus on comfort, these frames are easy to wear all day, every day.
