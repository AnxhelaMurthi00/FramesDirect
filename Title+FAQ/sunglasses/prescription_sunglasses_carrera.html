Carrera Prescription Sunglasses for Men, Women & Children

Carrera’s passion for innovation shows in this collection of prescription sunglasses that lives at the intersection of form and function. Wilhelm Anger named the company after the Carrera Panamericana—widely considered the most dangerous race on the planet—because he wanted his brand always to be on the edge, always pushing the boundaries of fashion and technology. First, that meant crafting ski racing goggles using patented Optyl, a material that was lighter, more durable and more adaptive to the wearer's face than anything the world had ever seen. He didn’t stop there, and a bold line of designer eyewear was born. Prescription lenses fine-tune these Rx-ready Carrera frames to your vision needs, in a style that perfectly suits your tastes.

Carrera Prescription Sunglass Frames Styles

Carrera’s collection of prescription sunglasses includes the iconic aviator, sleek rectangle frames, contemporary thick-rimmed rounds, and futuristic double-bridge styles. Prescription Lens Features and Tints

Add lenses in your Rx to Carrera’s prescription-ready frames for crisp, clear vision and protection from the sun. A number of lens colors are available; grey, green, and bronze lenses come polarized or non-polarized, and with or without an anti-reflective treatment.
